Solheimajokull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Myrdalsjokull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most gran volcanoes and during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms in the front of the glacier only adding to the charm of the loaction. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name Ice-land!
